Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Memora processes information when you use the mobile app, web support pages and related services. By using Memora, you agree to the practices described here.

No required account Ads supported Analytics used Firebase services

Data We Process

Memora may process the following categories of information:

  • App interactions, such as selected game modes, scores, accuracy, progress, levels, sessions and feature usage.
  • Device and app information, such as device identifiers, app version, platform, language setting and diagnostic information.
  • Advertising and analytics information, such as ad impressions, ad performance events and aggregated usage trends.
  • Content loading information for app features, including image assets used in 4 Pics 1 Word style puzzles.

Memora does not require users to create an account. If account-based features are added later, this policy will be updated before or when those features become available.

How We Use Data

  • To operate memory games, attention drills, logic games, math exercises, word puzzles, IQ tests and EQ reports.
  • To show progress, scores, levels, streaks and skill reports.
  • To improve app stability, design, difficulty balancing and feature quality.
  • To measure advertising performance and keep the app available as an ad-supported experience.
  • To detect errors, abuse, fraud or security issues.

Ads, Analytics and Third-Party Services

Memora may use Firebase services and Google Mobile Ads. These services may process identifiers, usage data, diagnostics and advertising information according to their own terms and privacy policies.

  • Firebase Analytics may be used to understand app usage and performance.
  • Firebase Storage may be used to deliver image assets and app content.
  • Google Mobile Ads may be used to display ads and measure ad performance.

Depending on your device, operating system and region, you may be able to limit ad tracking or reset your advertising identifier from your device settings.

Your Choices and Rights

You can stop app data processing by uninstalling Memora. You can also manage device-level permissions, analytics preferences where available, and advertising choices from your operating system settings.

To request access, correction or deletion of data that may be associated with you, use the developer contact channel shown on the app store listing where you downloaded Memora.

Children's Privacy

Memora is a general audience brain training and puzzle app. It is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us through the app store developer contact channel.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features, legal requirements or third-party services change. The latest version will be published on this page with an updated date.

Reklam, Analitik ve Ucuncu Taraf Hizmetleri

Memora, Firebase hizmetlerini ve Google Mobile Ads'i kullanabilir. Bu hizmetler kendi kosullari ve gizlilik politikalarina gore tanimlayicilar, kullanim verileri, tanilama bilgileri ve reklam bilgilerini isleyebilir.

  • Firebase Analytics, uygulama kullanimi ve performansini anlamak icin kullanilabilir.
  • Firebase Storage, gorsel varliklari ve uygulama iceriklerini sunmak icin kullanilabilir.
  • Google Mobile Ads, reklam gostermek ve reklam performansini olcmek icin kullanilabilir.

Cihaziniza, isletim sisteminize ve bolgenize bagli olarak reklam izlemeyi sinirlandirabilir veya reklam kimliginizi cihaz ayarlarindan sifirlayabilirsiniz.

Tercihleriniz ve Haklariniz

Memora'yi kaldirarak uygulama tarafindaki veri islemeyi durdurabilirsiniz. Ayrica cihaz izinlerini, varsa analitik tercihlerini ve reklam seceneklerini isletim sistemi ayarlarinizdan yonetebilirsiniz.

Sizinle iliskilendirilebilecek verilere erisim, duzeltme veya silme talebinde bulunmak icin Memora'yi indirdiginiz uygulama magazasindaki gelistirici iletisim kanalini kullanabilirsiniz.

Cocuklarin Gizliligi

Memora genel kitleye yonelik bir zihin egzersizi ve bulmaca uygulamasidir. Cocuklardan bilerek kisisel bilgi toplamak amaciyla tasarlanmamistir. Bir cocugun kisisel bilgi sagladigini dusunuyorsaniz uygulama magazasindaki gelistirici iletisim kanalindan bize ulasabilirsiniz.
